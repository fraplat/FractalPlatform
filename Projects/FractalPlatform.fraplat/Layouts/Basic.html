How It Works

Straightforward process, powerful results

Let Fractal Platform generate most of your website automatically — customize only what you need:

  • Create json collections of your data model - your database level
  • Fractal automatically creates CRUD business logic - your business logic level
  • Customize your business logic to your purposes - your business logic level
  • Fractal automatically generates a UI based on your data model and allows you to apply aspect-oriented logic to the interface - your user interface level
  • Customize your UI with AI — or do it manually with custom html where unique design matters - your user interface level
  • Keeps the door open to extend your project for more complex scenarios

How to Get Started

Create your unique web site on your own

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Open Fractal Studio in web browser
  • Select a template for your first app, such as HelloWorld or ToDoList
  • Review and customize your app’s code
  • Click the Deploy button and see your web project live
